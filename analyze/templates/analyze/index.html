about us

Dementia is a general term for decline in mental ability severe enough to interfere with daily life. It robs all mental faculties of the affected, progressively rendering the person completely dependent on others for even the most basic activities of daily life.

1

According to The World Alzheimer Report 2015 led by King's College London, India is home to 4.1 million cases of Dementia.

2

As per survey, several hospitals lack the facility of dementia detection and thus the disease is detected in a conventional way using MRI, mental status tests.

3

Due to lack of awareness regarding Dementia in India, there is a need for a diagnostic tool to predict and hence try to prevent it.
